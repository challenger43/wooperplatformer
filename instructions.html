This is our little blue hero, Sooper the Wooper:

Sooper's got one goal: visit his Paldean relatives. Unfortunately, Sooper's idea of “planning” involves forgetting half the details. He remembers something about needing Woop-Balls... but that's about it. Along the way, he keeps getting distracted and totally forgets why he’s on this trip in the first place. He’ll need to collect enough Woop Balls to turn into Quagsire (because apparently, that’s important). And...that's about it. Hopefully(read: 1% chance) he'll somehow make it to Paldea!

How To Play:

  • Use WASD or arrow keys to move. To jump, you can use either up-arrow, w, or spacebar
  • Collect all the Woop-Balls to unlock a portal that will take you to the next level!
  • All the levels are possible(as long as you have a brain) without needing to restart!
  • Once you unlock the quagsire form, you will be able to swim! Press P to toggle between Quagsire/Wooper
